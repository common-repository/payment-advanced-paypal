== Description ==

Accept Credit card payments using Paypal directly on your WooCommerce site.

Embed payment form directly in the checkout page.

Option to receive an email notification of failed transactions (to help detect fraud).

Choose between Authorization and Capture, or Authorization only and Capture later (when you are ready to ship).

You can choose the type of credit card you accept and their name & logo will automatically appear in the payment selection page (in new page mode).

Developed by Presto-Changeo.com, offering e-commerce modules and plugins since 2008

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to configure the module
After the module has been installed, navigate to the WooCommerce->Payments page.
Select PayPal Advanced and enter your PayPal account credentials.
